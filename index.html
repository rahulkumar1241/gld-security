About Us

Who We Are

GLD Security S.à.r.l. is a licensed security company founded in December 2011 in Luxembourg. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted names in professional security services across the Grand Duchy.

We adapt to every need and mission you entrust to us. Our competent, professional, and friendly personnel ensures every security assignment is handled with the highest level of quality, flexibility, loyalty, and above all — discretion.

A well-organised relationship between GLD Security, our clients, and our security agents is the foundation of our exceptional service delivery.
